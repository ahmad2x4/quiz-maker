Find the perimeter of this composite shape consisting of a rectangle (8 m × 5 m) with a semicircle on one of the shorter sides (diameter 5 m). Give your answer correct to 1 decimal place.

A composite shape consists of a rectangle 16 m × 10 m with two semicircles attached to opposite shorter sides (diameter 10 m each). Calculate the total area correct to 1 decimal place. (Use $\pi \approx 3.14$)

</details>

## Question 24

**Topic:** Measurement • **Difficulty:** Hard • **Points:** 4

A water tank is in the shape of a cylinder with radius 1.2 m and height 2.5 m. It is currently filled to 75% capacity. If a pump removes water at 15 L/min, how long will it take to empty the tank completely? Give your answer in hours, correct to 1 decimal place. (Use $\pi \approx 3.14$)

**Options:**
- A) 3.8 hours
- B) 5.0 hours
- C) 7.1 hours
- D) 9.4 hours

<details>
<summary>Show Explanation</summary>
**Correct Answer: D) 9.4 hours**


### Solution

**Step 1:** Calculate total tank volume:
$$V = \pi r^2 h = 3.14 \times 1.2^2 \times 2.5$$
$$V = 3.14 \times 1.44 \times 2.5 = 11.304 \text{ m}^3$$

**Step 2:** Convert to litres:
$$11.304 \times 1000 = 11\,304 \text{ L}$$

**Step 3:** Calculate current volume (75% full):
$$11\,304 \times 0.75 = 8\,478 \text{ L}$$

**Step 4:** Calculate time to empty:
$$\text{Time} = \frac{8\,478}{15} = 565.2 \text{ minutes}$$

**Step 5:** Convert to hours:
$$565.2 \div 60 = 9.42 \approx 9.4 \text{ hours}$$

</details>
