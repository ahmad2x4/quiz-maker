## Question 1

**Topic:** Algebra • **Difficulty:** Medium • **Points:** 10

The cost of a taxi trip is $3.80 plus $2.30 per kilometre. Which expression gives the total cost of a $d$ km trip?

**Options:**
- A) $3.80 + 2.30d$
- B) $3.80d + 2.30$
- C) $(3.80 + 2.30)d$
- D) $2.30 + 3.80d$

<details>
<summary>Show Explanation</summary>

**Correct Answer: A) $3.80 + 2.30d$**

### Solution

The fixed cost is $3.80. For each km travelled, $2.30 is added. So for $d$ km:

$$\text{Total} = 3.80 + 2.30 \times d = 3.80 + 2.30d$$

</details>

## Question 8

**Topic:** Algebra • **Difficulty:** Medium • **Points:** 10

**True or False:** Sonja wrote $7a + 3a = 10a^2$. This equation is correct.

**Options:**
- A) True
- B) False

<details>
<summary>Show Explanation</summary>
**Correct Answer: B) False**


### Solution

$7a$ and $3a$ are **like terms** (same variable $a$), so we add the coefficients — we do **not** add the exponents:

$$7a + 3a = 10a$$

The correct result is $10a$, not $10a^2$.

**Verify** by substituting $a = 5$:
- LHS: $7(5) + 3(5) = 35 + 15 = 50$
- RHS (Sonja's): $10(5^2) = 10 \times 25 = 250$

LHS $\neq$ RHS, so Sonja's equation is **False**.

</details>

## Question 19

**Topic:** Algebra • **Difficulty:** Medium • **Points:** 10

Factorise $8y + 16$.

**Options:**
- A) $4(2y + 4)$
- B) $8(y + 2)$
- C) $2(4y + 8)$
- D) $8(y + 16)$

<details>
<summary>Show Explanation</summary>
**Correct Answer: B) $8(y + 2)$**


### Solution

Find the HCF of $8y$ and $16$: the HCF is **8**.

$$8y + 16 = 8 \times y + 8 \times 2 = 8(y + 2)$$

**Check** by expanding: $8(y + 2) = 8y + 16$ ✓

Note: Option A, $4(2y + 4)$, is only partially factorised — 4 is still a common factor inside the brackets. Similarly, option C, $2(4y + 8)$, is also only partially factorised — a further factor of 4 can still be taken out of the bracket.

</details>

## Question 20

**Topic:** Algebra • **Difficulty:** Medium • **Points:** 10

Find the Highest Common Factor (HCF) of $24x^2$ and $16x$.

*(Enter your answer as a term, e.g. `8x`)*

**Answer:** $8x$

<details>
<summary>Show Explanation</summary>

### Solution

**Step 1** — HCF of the coefficients: $\text{HCF}(24,\ 16) = 8$

**Step 2** — HCF of the variables: $\text{HCF}(x^2,\ x) = x$ (take the lowest power)

**Step 3** — Multiply together:

$$\text{HCF} = 8x$$

**Check:** $24x^2 \div 8x = 3x$ and $16x \div 8x = 2$ — both divide evenly. ✓

</details>
